# Summary Prompt — produce a one-line summary

You will receive a transcript sample (front + tail of the original text).

## Your task

Produce **one single-line summary** that conveys the document's purpose and
main takeaways.

## Rules

- Length: **3–6 sentences, 200–500 Chinese characters total**.
- **One single line**: no line breaks anywhere. Use periods/commas to separate
  sentences; do not press Enter.
- Plain text only. No markdown, no quotes, no leading prefix like `Summary:`
  or `概要：`.
- Cover: (1) main topic, (2) key arguments or content areas, (3) conclusion
  or actionable takeaway if present.

## Output

Just the summary text on one line. Nothing else.
